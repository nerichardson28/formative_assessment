What are the Pros of Formative Feedback?

1.) Provides real-time feedback: this is helpful because teacher's can get real time information on student understanding and adjust teaching method and curriculum accordingly

2.) Supports personalized learning: helpful for identifying students who may be struggling and develope strategies to help them in the learning process

3.) Increases student engagement: encourages students to participate in activities and pay attention in class as they recieve ongoing feedback on said activities
