Examples of Formative Assessment

There are many examples of Formative Assessment that can be used. Some of these examples are:

Jigsaw:Students become "experts" on a small piece of information and then teach it to their group.

Exit slip: At the end of a lesson, ask students to write down one or two key takeaways or one question they still have on a small slip of paper.

Draft review:Provide feedback on unfinished work like an outline, a draft of an essay, or presentation slides to guide improvements before a final submission.

Digital polls or quizzes:Use tools to ask questions and get immediate, anonymous feedback on understanding
